Become An Agency Esports Sponsor

We offer a range of sponsorship opportunities for brands to align themselves with our innovative, engaging online tournmanents.

  • Connect with creative professionals and agency-side decision makers
  • Support the growth of an innovative new professional network
  • Align your company as a fun, forward-thinking and charitable brand

Your sponsorship will be used to directly cover software and technology costs critical for running events, arranging awesome professionally produced livestreams to cover grand finals, and providing thank-you packages to our volunteer staff.

10% of all sponsorships will also be put directly into the charity prize pool for which teams will be competing.

Contact us today to discuss becoming an Agency Esports sponsor. We welcome all enquiries and can tailor bespoke packages to suit all budget sizes and would be happy to have a chat to discuss these with you further.
