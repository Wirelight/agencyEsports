FIFA 21 Team Registration

Overview

The Afterwork.gg FIFA 21 Spring 2021 tournament is open for team registration for players on Xbox and Playstation.


This is a solo tournament, however, companies are permitted to enter multiple players. Entrants must have an active Xbox Live Gold or PS Plus subscription.

Tournament Format

The tournament takes places over two stages. First, there will be a round-robin group stage where 3 points are awarded for a win and 1 point for a draw.


Matches will consist of 2 fixtures with teams playing 2 matches per event day. The tournament then progresses into a single-elimination knockout.


All matches will be played on Fridays and to avoid conflict with most working hours the first match of each event day will begin at 18:00.


Final match times will be anounced once team registration closes and before the season begins once we know the exact number of teams. This tournament format section will be updated along with an announcement email for all entrants.


Entry Fees

Afterwork.gg team entry fees include tournament management, access to our Discord community, branding and advertising opportunities such as company logos and backlinks, player profiles and other promotion.


Additionally, we guarantee at least 50% of registration fees will be contributed to the charity prize pot for the tournament.


The final prize pot will be donated to the top three teams chosen charities using a 50%/30%/20% split.
