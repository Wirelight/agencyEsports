Call of Duty: Warzone Team Registration

Overview

The Agency Esports CoD: Warzone Spring 2021 tournament is open for team registration for players on PC, Xbox and Playstation.


A minimum of 3 players are required to form a team and enter under their company name. Companies are permitted to enter multiple teams. Teams wishing to enter with fewer than 3 players should contact Agency Esports to be matched up with other players in a similar position.

Tournament Format

The tournament takes places over a single League stage where teams will compete in one match per game day. Matches will consist of teams of 3 vs 3 competing in 3 rounds. Round 1 and 3 will use the standard Battle Royale gamemode and round 2 will use the Plunder gamemode.


Points will be awarded to teams on a sliding scale based on their scoreboard position at the close of each round with the maximum points available being equal to the total number of teams. For example, in a 20 team round the team in first place will be awarded 20 points. Additional points are awarded based on kills in BR and cash totals in Plunder.


A total of five matches will be played in the League. All matches will be played on Saturdays during the tournament period with the first match of each event day beginning at 15:00. Final placements will be determined by aggregate scorelines at the conclusion of all 5 matches.


Final match details will be anounced once team registration closes and before the season begins once we know the exact number of teams. This tournament format section will be updated along with an announcement email for all team captains.


Entry Fees

Agency Esports team entry fees include tournament management, access to our Discord community, branding and advertising opportunities such as company logos and backlinks, player profiles and other promotion.


Additionally, we guarantee at least 50% of registration fees will be contributed to the charity prize pot for the tournament.


The final prize pot will be donated to the top three teams chosen charities using a 50%/30%/20% split.


Ready To Enter?

Register your team using the entry form on this page. Payment of entry fees is not required at the point of registration.


After submission, the Team Captain will be sent an invoice for the registration fees due to be paid before the tournament commences.


Whilst we have tried to make the information here as clear as possible, please reach out should you any assistance or further information.


Finally, please remember that we expect every participant in Agency Esports tournaments to behave with respect for players, opponents and staff. Remember, you're representing your company and chosen charity as well as yourself so remain courteous, chivalrous and be a good sport. GLHF!
